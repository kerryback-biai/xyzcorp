# XYZ Corp Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Company Overview

XYZ Corp is a B2B industrial supplies distributor with approximately $500M in annual revenue. The company operates through three divisions: Industrial, Energy, and Safety, with a corporate headquarters providing shared services.

## Expense Policy

### Travel Expenses
- Domestic airfare must be booked at least 14 days in advance and in economy class
- Hotel stays are reimbursed up to $200/night for domestic travel, $300/night international
- Per diem for meals: $75/day domestic, $100/day international
- Rental cars require pre-approval for trips under 100 miles from the office

### Purchase Approvals
- Purchases under $500: department manager approval
- Purchases $500–$5,000: director approval
- Purchases $5,000–$25,000: VP approval
- Purchases over $25,000: CFO approval

## Return & Refund Policy

### Customer Returns
- Standard return window: 30 days from delivery for stock items
- Custom/fabricated items: non-returnable unless defective
- Restocking fee: 15% for returns of non-defective stock items
- Defective items: full refund or replacement, no restocking fee
- Returns over $5,000 require regional manager authorization
- All returns must include original packing slip or order number

### Vendor Returns
- Damaged goods must be reported within 48 hours of receipt
- Vendor RMA required before returning any items
- Freight damage claims must include photographic evidence

## IT Security Policy

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Multi-factor authentication required for all systems with customer data

### Data Classification
- **Confidential**: customer financial data, employee PII, pricing agreements
- **Internal**: operational reports, internal memos, org charts
- **Public**: marketing materials, product catalogs, press releases

## PTO Policy

- 0–2 years tenure: 15 days PTO
- 3–5 years tenure: 20 days PTO
- 6+ years tenure: 25 days PTO
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum carryover: 5 days into the next calendar year

## Vendor Management

### Approved Vendor List
All new vendors must go through the vendor qualification process:
1. Complete vendor application form
2. Provide proof of insurance (minimum $1M general liability)
3. Pass financial stability review
4. Sign standard terms and conditions
5. Quarterly performance reviews for vendors exceeding $100K annual spend

### Payment Terms
- Standard payment terms: Net 30
- Preferred vendors (>$500K annual): Net 45 available
- Early payment discount: 2/10 Net 30 where offered
- All invoices must reference a valid PO number
